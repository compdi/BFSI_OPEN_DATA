BFSI Open Data

📊 India’s Public Insurance Data, Made Interactive

BFSI Open Data is an initiative by Compliance Digest to make public data from IRDAI and insurers more accessible, visual, and useful. This dashboard showcases insights from regulatory filings, public disclosures, and insurance performance data.

Whether you're from compliance, product, analytics, consulting, or academia — this dashboard offers ready visual access to sector trends.
